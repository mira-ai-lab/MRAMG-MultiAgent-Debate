{context}

3. Image Captions (Visual Content Reference):
{caption}
- Note: Each caption is sequentially aligned in a one-to-one correspondence with its respective image placeholder <img> found in the Context.

{text_draft}

5. Debate Ledger (Mandatory Modifications):
{debate_ledger}

# Task & Instructions
Take the "Original Text Draft" as your structural foundation and STRICTLY apply all decisions recorded in the "Debate Ledger". 

How to process the Ledger modifications:
- For SET/INCLUSION Conflicts marked "INCLUDE": Insert the specified image placeholder into the draft. Use the "Image Captions" and "Full Context" to find the most logically appropriate sentence. Write a natural, brief transitional phrase (e.g., "As illustrated in the diagram showing...") based on the caption.
- For SET/INCLUSION Conflicts marked "EXCLUDE": Remove the specified image placeholder from the draft completely. Smooth out any surrounding transitional words so the text remains natural.
- For ORDER/SEQUENTIAL Conflicts: You will be given a specific approved sequence (e.g., ["<img2>", "<img1>"]). You MUST rearrange the image placeholders in the text to strictly match this sequence. Adjust the surrounding text slightly if necessary to ensure the narrative flow aligns with this new causal/chronological order.

Constraints:
- Preserve the core narrative structure and wording of the Original Text Draft as much as possible. 
- Do NOT hallucinate or add new information outside the provided Context.
